about me

I am a full-stack web engineer student studying to complete my diploma by April 2025. My various work experiences and passion for technology have led me to a more rewarding career path.

I once hiked 120 miles in roughly 10 days, and looking back at that now, I think the only reason my flat feet and I survived is due to my sheer determination and clear intentions. I went into the experience bracing myself for the difficulty ahead, both physically and emotionally, knowing it would be worth it to embrace every moment and appreciate the growing experience, even if it was incredibly difficult. Another incredibly difficult but worthwhile experience was being a high-school teacher. My teaching background honed my interpersonal skills as well as my ability to be flexible, train/teach others, and create curriculum. My administrative positions lead me to re-discover and develop my technical skills, which I quickly realized I had always been naturally skilled at ever since I was young and writing HTML to personalize my early 2000's blogs. This strength became clear again very quickly, particularly when I was tasked with implementing a new software system for my entire department as well as our students and community partners, including training all users. Due to my experiences, both professional and personal, I have developed into a competent, constantly evolving person with strong values, who embraces the present moment while also able to look ahead, problem solve, and work hard.
